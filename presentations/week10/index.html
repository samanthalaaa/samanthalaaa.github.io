About Me

I'm Tracey! I was born and raised in Columbus, Ohio and developed a love for travel after my first trip abroad to Ireland. I was eight and we visited my father's family for a few days and toured the countryside. Since then, I've always jumped at the chance to visit other places. My first solo trip came when I was 18 and I flew to Ibiza to work in a hostel there for two months. I got a TEFL certificate which allowed me to teach English to both children and adults in South Korea, the Philippines, Thailand, and Saudi Arabia. I've currently been away from Ohio for over two years, and while I miss home, I always meet people in every destination who make each new city feel like home.
